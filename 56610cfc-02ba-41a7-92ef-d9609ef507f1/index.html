About the Data

For international data, from B.C. = Emigrants + Net Temporary Emigrants – Returning Emigrants. To B.C. = International Immigrants + Net Non Permanant Residents.

International migration data source: Statistics Canada. Table 051-0037 - International migration components, Canada, provinces and territories, quarterly (persons), CANSIM (database).

Inter-provincial migration data source: Statistics Canada. Table 051-0045 - Interprovincial migrants, by province or territory of origin and destination, quarterly (persons), CANSIM (database).
